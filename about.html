About

A digital exploration of the rich history and breathtaking beauty of Daraga, Albay! Created as part of our Reading in Philippine History course, this blog delves into five remarkable landmarks that define Daraga’s cultural and natural heritage: the iconic Daraga Church, the hauntingly beautiful Cagsawa Ruins, the lesser-known yet historically significant Budiao Ruins Church, the majestic Mayon Volcano, and the charming Farmplate Daraga. Through this platform, we aim to share fascinating stories, historical insights, and the enduring significance of these sites—not just as tourist spots, but as vital pieces of the Philippines' past and present.

Our blog is more than just an academic project; it’s a tribute to Daraga’s legacy. Whether you're a history enthusiast, a traveler, or a fellow student, we hope our posts inspire you to appreciate these wonders and perhaps even visit them someday. Join us as we uncover the tales behind these landmarks and celebrate the beauty of our heritage!
